# Hon. Linda Somiari-Stewart Delivers on Tech Hub Beneficiaries Promise

In a strong demonstration of her commitment to youth development and digital empowerment, Hon. Linda Somiari-Stewart, Deputy Leader of the Rivers State House of Assembly and Member representing Okrika Constituency, has fulfilled her promise to beneficiaries of her Tech Scholarship Program at the Harvoxx Tech Hub 2025 program.

## Equipment Distribution Ceremony

At a well-attended handover event, beneficiaries received brand-new laptops and MiFi devices with active internet subscriptions, equipping them with the essential tools required to thrive in today's digital economy. The intervention is aimed at bridging the digital divide, promoting innovation, and empowering Okrika youths with practical technology skills.

## Personal Oversight and Accountability

Known for her proactive and people-focused leadership, Hon. Somiari-Stewart personally supervised the distribution process, ensuring that every participant received the promised resources to continue their tech journey without disruption.

## Vision for Youth Empowerment

"Empowering our youth is not just a promise; it is an investment in Okrika's brighter future. These tools and certifications will open doors to endless opportunities," Hon. Linda Somiari-Stewart stated during the ceremony.

## Positioning Okrika for the Future

The initiative reinforces Okrika's growing position as a hub for technological advancement while setting a strong example of accountable leadership and promise-keeping. With laptops in hand and certificates earned, the beneficiaries are now well-positioned to code, connect, innovate, and contribute meaningfully to community development.

Hon. Linda Somiari-Stewart's intervention stands as a clear testament to her dedication to youth empowerment, digital inclusion, and sustainable development within Okrika Constituency.

## The Road Ahead

This program represents just one step in Hon. Somiari-Stewart's broader vision of transforming Okrika into a technology-driven community where young people have the tools and skills they need to succeed in the 21st century.
