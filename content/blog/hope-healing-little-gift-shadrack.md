# Hon. Linda Somiari-Stewart Brings Hope and Healing to Little Gift Shadrack

In a remarkable act of compassion and service, Hon. Linda Somiari-Stewart, DSSRS, Deputy Leader of the Rivers State House of Assembly, personally ensured that little Gift Shadrack, diagnosed with a serious kidney condition, received full medical care and support.

Thanks to her intervention, Gift can now walk, eat, and function normally, bringing immense joy to his family and community. Beyond addressing the child's medical needs, Hon. Somiari-Stewart also empowered his mother, providing her with the resources to support her family sustainably.

## A Testament to Compassionate Leadership

"True leadership is measured not only by laws passed or projects delivered but by the lives touched and hope restored," said community members in admiration of her selfless efforts.

This extraordinary act reflects Hon. Linda Somiari-Stewart's dedication, empathy, and commitment to the people of Okrika Constituency, proving that effective leadership goes hand-in-hand with genuine care for the vulnerable.

## Impact on the Community

The intervention not only saved a young life but also demonstrated the power of compassionate governance. By addressing both the immediate medical crisis and the long-term economic stability of the family, Hon. Somiari-Stewart showed that true representation means caring for the most vulnerable members of our society.

This act of kindness serves as an inspiration to all leaders and reminds us that every life matters, and every child deserves the opportunity to thrive.

*Date of Intervention: 14 March*
