# Hon. Linda Stewart Empowers Okrika Youth, Hands Over Ogoloma Youth Development Centre

Hon. Linda Somiari Stewart, the Honourable Member representing Okrika Constituency in the Rivers State House of Assembly, has reaffirmed her commitment to youth empowerment with the successful handover of the Ogoloma Youth Development Centre to the people of Okrika.

## A Hub for Youth Development

The centre, designed as a hub for youth development and capacity building, will serve as a platform for skills acquisition, leadership training, community engagement, and empowerment programs targeted at young people across the constituency. The initiative is part of Hon. Linda's broader agenda to invest in the future of Okrika by equipping youths with opportunities for growth and self-reliance.

## Vision for Sustainable Development

Speaking at the handover ceremony, Hon. Linda emphasized that sustainable development begins with empowering young people and creating safe spaces where innovation, learning, and collaboration can thrive. She noted that the Ogoloma Youth Development Centre will play a vital role in nurturing talents, reducing unemployment, and fostering positive community participation.

## Community Impact and Recognition

Community leaders and youth representatives expressed gratitude for the project, describing it as timely and impactful. They commended Hon. Linda for prioritizing youth-focused infrastructure that addresses real needs and delivers long-term value to the constituency.

## A Testament to People-Oriented Leadership

The Ogoloma Youth Development Centre stands as another testament to Hon. Linda Somiari Stewart's dedication to people-oriented representation, community development, and transformative leadership in Okrika Constituency.

## Programs and Opportunities

The centre will offer a comprehensive range of programs designed to meet the diverse needs of Okrika's youth:

- **Skills Acquisition**: Vocational training and technical skills development
- **Leadership Training**: Programs to develop future community leaders
- **Community Engagement**: Initiatives to promote civic participation and social responsibility
- **Entrepreneurship Support**: Resources and mentorship for young business owners

## Long-Term Vision

By investing in youth infrastructure, Hon. Somiari Stewart is laying the foundation for a brighter future where Okrika's young people are equipped to drive economic growth, innovation, and positive change in their communities.

This facility represents not just a building, but a commitment to the next generation and a strategic investment in Okrika's human capital.
